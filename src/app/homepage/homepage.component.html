About Hackathon

Due to traditional method of recruitment, time limit and other factors(like grade of college, etc.), many colleges remain unvisited. With this type of approach, organization cannot give chance to each student for proving himself/herself. Thus, it becomes difficult for organization to identify talent from every college. Also, due to the current pandemic situation, it is nearly impossible to conduct such recruiment drives in any college.

All these difficulties can be easily solved with the help of online recruitment portal 'Hackathon'. Hackathon provides service of online recruitment exams for placement drives. With hackathon, organizations can set their difficulty according to the levels of exam.

Thus, Hackathon makes it easier for organizations to conduct their recruitment drives with broad scope and also provides equal opportunity to each and every candidate to appear for drives.
