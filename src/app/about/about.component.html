About Hackathon

Hackathon makes it easy to connect with students and begin doing great work.

This website is developed for students, companies or any organization to conduct any examinations from this site. Hackathon reduces gap between these companies or any organization and such potential candidates this platform will help the deserving candidates to showcase their conceptual skills through an online exam.




How Hackathon works

  • Hackathon allows you to test your knowlege across various subjects as per the requirement.

  • Each subject is categorized into 3 levels starting from level 1. The difficulty of the exams increases as the levels increase.

  • Each test is timed and digitally proctored for Added Peace Of Mind

  • We generate detailed and instant analytics on each student after each exam is taken.

  • We allow coustomisation aspects of the test, giving you complete control
